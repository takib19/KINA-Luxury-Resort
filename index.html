ABOUT US.

Jules Coffee was founded by two 15 year olds named Julia and Rules in 2018. The first six months they were selling coffee on the street from a foldable table. Quickly, they received lots of praise from the general public. A year later, Julia and Rules opened their first physical location and the rest is history.
